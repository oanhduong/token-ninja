# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, personal
characteristics, or technical preference.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith.
- Credit others' work.
- Disagree on ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussion, issues, or PRs.

## Enforcement

Report violations to `conduct@token-ninja.dev` or open a private security
advisory on GitHub. Maintainers will respond within 7 days. Consequences
range from a private warning to a permanent ban, proportional to the
behavior.

Full text: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
